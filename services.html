Services provided in our store

personalized Styling Assistance: Staff may offer advice on dress selection based on the customer's body type, occasion, and style preferences.
Fitting and Alterations: Many dress shops offer fitting services and alterations to ensure that the dress fits perfectly. This may include hemming, resizing, or adjustments to the neckline and waist.
Custom Dress Design: Some dress shops offer bespoke or made-to-order dresses, where customers can collaborate with designers to create a unique dress tailored to their specifications.
Dress Rental: Some stores offer dress rental services, particularly for special occasions like proms, weddings, or galas, where customers can rent high-quality dresses for a limited period.
Gift Wrapping: For special occasions, the shop may provide gift wrapping services, allowing customers to purchase a dress as a gift with a decorative presentation.
Delivery and Shipping: Many dress shops offer delivery services, either local or international, so that customers can have their dresses shipped to their home or a location of their choice. Online Shopping Assistance: Some dress shops offer online shopping with easy navigation, providing virtual consultations or detailed size guides to assist with online purchases.
